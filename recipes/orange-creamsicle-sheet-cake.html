Orange Creamsicle Sheet Cake

Description:

This Orange Creamsicle Sheet Cake is a delightful dessert that combines the refreshing flavors of orange and vanilla. It's perfect for summer gatherings or any occasion where you want to impress your guests with a light and fruity treat.

Prep Time:

20 minutes

Cook Time:

30 minutes

Cool Time:

15 minutes

Total Time:

1 hour 5 minutes

Servings:

12 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 box of white or yellow cake mix
  • 1 cup of orange juice
  • 1/3 cup of vegetable oil
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 orange, zested
  • 1 cup of whipped cream for topping
  • 1 package of instant orange jello
  • 2 cup of boiling water
  • 1 package of vanilla pudding mix
  • 1 cups of milk
  • 1 8oz tub of Cool Whip, thawed

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Spray a 9x13-inch pan with nonstick cooking spray. Set aside.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cake mix, orange juice, vegetable oil, eggs, vanilla extract, and orange zest.
  3. Mix until well combined and pour into a greased 9x13 inch baking pan.
  4. Bake for 30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  5. Using the handle of a wooden spoon, poke holes throughout cake.
  6. Add orange jello to boiling water and stir until dissolved. Pour jello mixture over cake. Refrigerate until cool, 2 to 3 hours.
  7. Whisk vanilla pudding mix and milk together until thickened, about 2 minutes. Fold in Cool Whip.
  8. Spread mixture over cooled cake and refrigerate overnight.
  9. Serve and enjoy your Orange Creamsicle Sheet Cake!
