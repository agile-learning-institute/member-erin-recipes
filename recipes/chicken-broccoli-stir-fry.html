Chicken and Broccoli Stir Fry

Description

A quick and healthy stir fry with tender chicken and vibrant broccoli, perfect for a weeknight dinner.

Prep Time:

10 minutes

Cook Time:

15 minutes

Total Time:

25 minutes

Servings:

2 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts, sliced
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice or noodles for serving

Directions

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Add sliced chicken and cook until browned.
  4. Add broccoli florets and stir-fry for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Pour in soy sauce, season with salt and pepper, and stir well.
  6. Serve hot over cooked rice or noodles.
